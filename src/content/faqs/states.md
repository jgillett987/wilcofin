---
question: Do I have to live in Tennessee to be a client?
category: Working With Us
order: 60
---

No. Wilco Financial is registered as an investment adviser with the State of Tennessee, and we can work with clients in other states where we are registered, have filed notice, or are otherwise exempted from registration.

If you're unsure whether we can work together in your state, [reach out](/contact) and we'll let you know directly.
